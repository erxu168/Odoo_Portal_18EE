## Press Release

*Use for press pitches + posting on website news section.*

---

**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**
**Berlin, [dd] May 2026**

### What a Jerk — Berlin's first proper Jamaican quick-service restaurant — opens May 14 on Kottbusser Damm

**WHAT:** What a Jerk, a new Jamaican quick-service restaurant, opens its doors on Thursday, May 14, 2026, at Kottbusser Damm 96 in Berlin.

**WHERE:** Kottbusser Damm 96, 10967 Berlin-Kreuzberg (Hermannplatz side)

**WHEN:** Soft opening Thursday, May 14. Grand opening weekend May 22–23. Open daily from 12:00, Friday and Saturday until 01:00.

**WHO:** Founded by Ethan Xu, the restaurateur behind Ssam Korean BBQ on the same street. Chinese-born and Berlin-based, Xu spent years eating through the Jamaican food scenes of Vancouver and Toronto — the two largest Jamaican diaspora food cities in North America — before opening What a Jerk.

**WHY IT MATTERS:** Berlin has fewer than a handful of dedicated Jamaican restaurants, and none on Kottbusser Damm, one of the city's busiest food corridors. What a Jerk fills a clear gap: real jerk chicken, real scotch-bonnet heat, and quick-service speed that fits the neighborhood's daily rhythm.

**THE FOOD:** Signature jerk chicken wraps (€8.50), oxtail stew (€15), curry goat, brown stew chicken, jackfruit stew (vegan), Jamaican patties (€3.50), and traditional sides — festival, rice & peas, escovitch pickled vegetables, fried plantain. Scotch-bonnet heat is the default; diners can dial it up or down.

**DELIVERY:** Available on Wolt and Uber Eats from opening day.

**PRESS CONTACT:** [Name] — [email] — [phone]
**IMAGES:** High-res photos, menu, and owner portrait available on request.
**TASTINGS:** Press tastings available May 5–17 by appointment.
