## Do / Don't Language Cheatsheet

**USE:**
- Fire, heat, smoky, jerk, bold, fresh, filling, flavorful
- Ya mon, one love (sparingly, never corporate-speak)
- "Pick your heat"
- "Kotti Damm"
- Bilingual mix is fine: "Neu am Kotti. Coming in hot."

**AVOID:**
- "Ethnic" / "exotic"
- "Authentic taste of the islands"
- "Experience the flavors of…"
- Exclamation points (use sparingly — once per post max)
- Fake heritage claims (no "grandma's recipe")
- Over-using patois — two phrases per post max
