# What a Jerk — Launch Plan

*Opening: ~Thursday, 14 May 2026 (3 weeks from 2026-04-23)*
*Location: Kottbusser Damm 96, 10967 Berlin*

---

## TL;DR

- **Soft open Thursday May 14** (midweek, lower stakes, shake out ops)
- **Grand opening weekend Fri May 22 / Sat May 23** (full press push, full heat)
- 3-week countdown builds anticipation on Instagram + Wolt + local press
- Primary growth engine: **Instagram + Ssam cross-promo + Berlin food press**
- Target month 1: **€3,000 daily sales, 1,000+ IG followers, featured in 2+ Berlin food outlets**

---

## Phased Approach (restaurant version)

| Phase | When | Who | Purpose |
|-------|------|-----|---------|
| **Internal (F&F tasting)** | Week -2 (~Apr 30) | 10–15 friends, family, staff | Shake down kitchen, test menu, get honest feedback |
| **Alpha (influencer preview)** | Week -1 (Mon–Wed May 5–7) | 5–10 local food creators + 1–2 journalists | Generate content + pre-opening press in exchange for free meal |
| **Beta (soft open)** | Thu–Sun May 14–17 | Public, but quietly announced on IG only | Break in ops, keep expectations low, collect early reviews |
| **Early access weekend** | Mon–Thu May 18–21 | Public, fuller menu, delivery live | Dial in speed + consistency before the press pile-on |
| **Grand opening** | Fri–Sat May 22–23 | Everyone. Press push. Ads live. | Maximum visibility + revenue |

> **Why phase it?** Your biggest enemy is "a great blogger comes on night 2, you're slammed, the jerk is dry, they write it up as mid." Soft-open so your 3-star nights happen before the press arrives.

### Opening Week: Thursday May 14 – Sunday May 17 (Soft Open)

**Vibe:** Open, take orders, don't hype. Let the food do the talking. Fix any ops issues immediately.

**Each day:**
- IG story at lunch: "We're open. Come through." + location tag
- IG story at dinner: "Still going. Wraps flying out."
- IG grid post only once (Thursday — opening day announcement)
- Collect diner photos (if they're happy to share, repost with tag)

**Specific ops targets (soft open):**
- Average ticket time under 6 min for wraps, under 10 min for dishes
- Wolt/Uber Eats rating must stay ≥ 4.5
- At least one staff member dedicated to taking photos / repost candidates each day

**If something breaks:** Turn off Wolt / close early / whatever. Better to be closed than to serve dry jerk.

## Risks & Contingencies

| Risk | Likelihood | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|------------|
| Wolt/UE approval delays past May 14 | High | Apply Week -3, launch dine-in only if needed |
| Construction/permit delays push opening | Medium | Have a "we're opening May 21 instead" version of every asset ready |
| Spice-level complaints from first-timers | High | Default to medium on wraps, only mention "full fire" if asked |
| Negative review on opening week (dry chicken, wait time) | High | Respond publicly + warmly within 4 hours, offer them back in |
| Bad weather opening weekend | Medium | Delivery push becomes primary; heavy IG Stories push |
| Going viral before ready | Low but serious | Have a "limit the menu to 4 items + cash only" fallback if ops break |
| Ssam regulars feel "abandoned" | Low | Cross-promo framing: "Ssam team adds a second location, not a replacement" |

## What NOT To Do

- **Don't do a big "grand opening" on Day 1.** Soft open, fix ops, then big push Week 2. First weekend is when a single bad review spreads.
- **Don't pay for influencers on opening week.** Gifted meals > sponsored posts — sponsored content reads as thirsty.
- **Don't discount on opening day.** Novelty is enough. Discounts attract the wrong crowd and anchor you low.
- **Don't run FB/IG ads the first 3 days.** Organic first — if the post engagement is poor, ads won't save it.
- **Don't neglect Google Business Profile.** 40% of your walk-in traffic will come from Google Maps searches. Photos + hours + menu must be complete before opening.
- **Don't let the kitchen outpace the front.** If Wolt is piling up + walk-ins are waiting, pause Wolt. Protect dine-in experience.
