## The 30-Second Pitch

**What a Jerk** is the first proper Jamaican quick-service spot on Kottbusser Damm. Jerk chicken wraps €8.50, curry goat, oxtail stew, patties. Real scotch-bonnet heat, dial-down optional. From the team behind Ssam Korean BBQ on the same street. Open daily from 12:00, Fri + Sat till 01:00. Delivery on Wolt + Uber Eats.

**Voice:** playful, welcoming, cool. Bilingual DE/EN. Sparing use of "ya mon" / "one love."

**Goals, month 1:** €3,000 daily sales · 1,000+ Instagram followers · 2+ press features · walk-ins as primary conversion.

## Principles (if you only remember three things)

1. **Food first.** No amount of marketing fixes mediocre food. Opening-week ops are the priority.
2. **Kotti Damm first.** You're a neighborhood spot. Tight radius, deep love, repeat visits > city-wide reach.
3. **The plate is the product, the post is the multiplier.** Every wrap that leaves the counter either becomes a post or doesn't — make it worth posting.

One love. 🔥
